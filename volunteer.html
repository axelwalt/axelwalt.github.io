Volunteer

We accept and encourage volunteer individuals or groups to join hands in the following projects:-


Young Women

CODEC Tanzania needs the volunteers to train on entrepreneurship skills and empowering young women in Hai and Siha District. Skilled young women will be able to say No to unsafe sex and underage pregnancies.

Join us

Daycare School Children's

To help in the dissemination of this education on the nutritious feed by nutrition potatoes. Many of the children mothers are young and employed so they don’t know how to well feed their children .so volunteers will help to bridge the gap.

Join us

Climate change

We need volunteers to help to teach primary school children from the age of 10 years to 13 years on the meaning of climate change and mitigation and adoptation. This education will start with the local government leaders in Siha and Hai district.

Join us

Nutrition project

In this project, we have demonstration plot with special kind of potatoes which when consumed they reduce the rate of malnutrition growth in human bodies this special species for people with malnutrition specifically children for the health boost. Because production of the potatoes do not use artificial fertilizer but organic we preserve the environment by keeping the soil and produce organic so we need the volunteers who can help in education on the need to use nutrition potatoes organically produced and also to help in expert advice in the farming.
