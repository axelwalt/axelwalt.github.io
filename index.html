We are Community Development and Enviroment Care Tanzania (CODECT)

We are committed to a sustainable future and to improving the social, economic and environmental well being of the community.

View Details

What we do

CODECT projects focus on: food security and nutrition, Primary Health education, Increase access to clean water and sanitary means, Stimulating households to dig a toilet, Education on HIV/AIDS prevention

Read more

Why we do it

CODECT aim to reduce poverty and suffering. create employment and economic opportunities. achieve social, economic, cultural and environmental goals. thorough training in Capacity Building.

Read more

Where we work

We are NGO registered in Tanzania with National wide Operational Area /Coverage we work with community and volunteers and partners around the world, we are based in Kilimanjaro region Hai district.
