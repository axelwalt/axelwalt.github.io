HUMAN RIGHTS AND GENDER EQUALITY PROGRAM

As the world today is striving to see the world where rights of the people are observed and that groups are not treated differently basing on their gender or any other differences sustainable vision (suvis) focuses on teaching the large community on the importance of gender equality and the benefits of equal treatment to socio-economic development.

Environmental conservation

Sustainable Vision (SUVIS) educates the community how to live without affecting the environment and making the world a safe place for every living creature. We teach the community to stop using wood as cooking energy, tree planting in water sources, stop poisoning, cultivating agriculture that does not affect the environment and also stop damaging water sources.
