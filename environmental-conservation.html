PROGRAMS


ENVIRONMENTAL



Sustainable Vision (SUVIS) educates the community how to live without affecting the environment and making the world a safe place for every living creature. We teach the community to stop using wood as cooking energy, tree planting in water sources, stop poisoning, cultivating agriculture that does not affect the environment and also stop damaging water sources.

As the World Urbanizes and industrializes, and as effects of climate change intensify, environmental crises will increasingly devastate the lives, health and livelihood of people around the global. A lack of legal regulation and enforcement of industrial and artisanal mining, large scale -dams, deforestation, domestic water , sanitation system and heavily polluting industries can lead to host of human violations. Activists and ordinary citizens defending their rights to land and the environment may face intimidation, legal harassment and deadly violence.

The primary victims of environment harm are often impoverished and marginalized community with limited opportunity to meaningfully participate in decision – making and public debate on environmental issues, and have little access to independent courts to achieve accountability and redress.
