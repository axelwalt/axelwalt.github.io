About Us

What is CODEC Tz

This is non Governmental organization registered in Tanzania with registration number 00NGO/00004083 to operate all over Tanzania mainland. CODEC Tz was established in 6 September, 2010 with the aim of educating the community on poverty, and how to pull out of it.

What we do

We run programs mostly training where local and foreign networks can provide hands on service while participating together with host communities and naturing understanding those contributing to the development of the nation through:

Environment care

Through discouraging trees felling, Planting of environment friendly trees species especially in water sources and Encouraging people to use alternative sources of energy other than firewood so that to minimize trees felling.

poverty alleviation

Through women and youth entrepreneurship skills and empowerment

Health care

Through mother child health, AIDS and Malaria

Nutrition

Through training on balanced foods such as nutritious potatoes. This kind of potato is best for young children because it boosts their health.

Climate Change / Adaptation and mitigation

Through mitigation adaptation and conservation activities

Care and support for the disadvantaged children and orphans

Due to AIDS death, domestic violence and underage pregnancies

Why

The purpose of establishing this organization was primarily to combat poverty through environment care, Health care, Climate change mitigation and adaptation, Training on enterpreneurship skills and empowerment to Women, young women and youth, Care for orphans and disadvantaged children.
